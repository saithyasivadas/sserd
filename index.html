About us

Society for Space Education Research and Development (SSERD) is an community devoted to elevate and promote space education and research all over the world and to do so, we have merged with numerous similar minded organizations across the globe. Together, we strive to make the knowledge of our mystifying celestial neighbours more accessible and the age old adage of "reach for the stars" more possible.
One of our main objectives is to kindle and develop the love for space science among the young. To sow the seeds of scientific curiosity deep enough, we expose students to astronomy and space technologies right from primary school level by conducting space camps, workshops and seminars. It is important to ignite and inspire young minds to contribute to the next generation space research. To empower them to do so, we also organize various projects at school and college levels.

Our services

We are full of exciting yet intriguing ways for young minds who is starving for space science.

Aerospace Workshop

Theoretical and hands on experience on Hydro & Chemical Rocketry, Aeromodelling, Space Robotics, Astronomy, Gliders etc.

Astro Space Camp

Spend your summers and winters with us in our Space Camp and make new acquaintances to enrich yourselves in the Space domain.

Sky Gazing

An opportunity to get a closer view of everyday objects in the sky like Moon, Planets, Stars, Constellation etc.

Scientists Interaction

One of the wonderful opportunity to interact with eminent Space Scientists from around the world.


Blog

A Platform to pen down the thoughts on any interesting topic which may or may not be realistic, but they surely are innovative.


Space Competitions

Annual Competitions on Model Rocketry, Lunar City Challenge, Aeromodelling, Space Olympiad, National level Article writing and many more.

Field Trips

A Visit to industries of space sectors to gain practical knowledge about the working of various components of Aircrafts, Helicopter, Space vehicles etc.

Internship and Projects

Monthly Internship and 3 months Projects are provided by SSERD on Space Topics.
